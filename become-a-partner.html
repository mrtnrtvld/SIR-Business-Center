Become a Partner

Society of Interventional Radiology (SIR) is committed to working with companies to provide our members with benefits that improve productivity, reduce operational costs, and increase the value of membership.

Our primary focus when working with partners is to ensure that members benefit from excellent service, outstanding products, and pricing. We work closely with all our partners to understand their brand and business objectives and ensure that members are aware of the services offered.
